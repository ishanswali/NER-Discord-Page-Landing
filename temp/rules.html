SERVER RULES AND BEST PRACTICES

1 — Who can access this server

No potential server member shall be excluded from participation in or subjected to discrimination on the basis of race, color, sex, religion, national origin, sexual orientation, gender identity or expression, or disability. In compliance with Discord's Terms of Service, server members must be at least 13 years of age. All potential server members must pass an eservices Civil Air Patrol membership check. Cadet members must have achieved the grade of C/Amn. Members found to be under the age of 13 or who let their Civil Air Patrol memberships lapse will be removed.

2 — Cadet Protection (CPP)

In compliance with Civil Air Patrol’s Cadet Protection Policy, server staff will not respond to any DMs from server members. Server members are strongly discouraged from starting DMs with each other in the spirit of Cadet Protection. All concerns about cadet protection should be brought to senior administrators through the Cadet Protection Policy reporting system.

3 — Operational Security (OPSEC)

Refrain from sharing your own personal information, or the personal information of others. Do not disclose Civil Air Patrol mission information.

4 — Behaviour

All Civil Air Patrol members should strive to embody the core values in their everyday actions. In adhering to the core value of respect, be respectful and inclusive in all chats and discussions on this server. In adhering to the core value of integrity, do not lie to or mislead others regarding your Civil Air Patrol grade, achievements, or experiences. Bullying/hate speech will not be tolerated and will result in a ban.

5 — Content

Appropriateness is subject to server staff discretion and is based on appropriateness for Civil Air Patrol activities. Staff will remove any content deemed inappropriate. Accounts with inappropriate profile pictures or Discord usernames will be kicked and require re-verification.

6 — Other Rules

  • Refrain from spamming
  • Keep discussions within the channel topic
  • No self-promotion or advertising
